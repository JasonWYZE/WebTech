Incrementing Social Media Followers

Use internal JavaScript to implement the following features:

  • When the webpage is loaded, the numbers of social media followers are changing from 0 to the target numbers (8000 Instagram followers and 5000 YouTube Subscribers).
  • Every 0.05 second, the two numbers should increase by a certain rate. The entire changing animation should be done within 2 seconds.
  • After reaching to the target numbers (in <= 2 seconds), changing animation should stop.
  • For full points, let two numbers reach to the targets at same time. (5 points)
